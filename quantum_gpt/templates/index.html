Getting Started:

  1. Registration: If you're new here, start by creating an account. Every new user is granted 20 free question tokens as a welcome gift!
  2. Ask Away: Pose your quantum-related questions. Remember, each question consumes one token.
  3. Feedback & Rewards: Found the response insightful? Or think there's room for improvement? Leave a comment! Your feedback is invaluable to us. For every constructive comment you provide, you'll earn 20 additional question tokens.
  4. Token Management: Keep an eye on your token count. Once you've used up your initial tokens, you can earn more by providing feedback. Ensure you don't exhaust all your tokens.
  5. Privacy First: We respect your privacy. Only questions and responses that receive comments are collected for further refinement of our model.

Commenting Guidelines:

Your feedback is instrumental in refining and enhancing Quantum GPT. To ensure that your comments are constructive and beneficial, please follow these guidelines:

Good Practices:

  • Be Specific: Clearly mention which part of the response you found accurate or inaccurate.
  • Provide Context: If possible, give a brief explanation of why you agree or disagree with the response.
  • Constructive Criticism: If you find an error or discrepancy in the response, suggest how it can be improved or corrected.
  • Stay Relevant: Ensure your comments pertain to the quantum-related question and response.
  • Politeness Counts: Remember, Quantum GPT is a tool in development. Approach feedback with a constructive and respectful tone.

What to Avoid:

  • Vague Feedback: Comments like "This is wrong" without any further explanation aren't helpful.
  • Personal Opinions: Avoid basing feedback solely on personal beliefs or unverified opinions.
  • Inappropriate Language: Refrain from using offensive or inappropriate language in your comments.
  • Multiple Comments: If you have additional feedback on a single response, try to consolidate it into one comment.
  • Spamming: Please don't spam the comment section with irrelevant content or promotional material.
